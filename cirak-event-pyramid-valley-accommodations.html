Pods

Pods are an affordable way to join the retreat while enjoying a shared, community experience. Six air-conditioned dorms house 18 pods each, separated by gender on different floors, each with three showers and three bathrooms cleaned daily. Note: Pods are not suitable if you’re taller than 1.87m (6’2″)

  • Individual pod with fan, power outlet, USB charging port
  • Comfortable mattress, pillow, and blanket provided
  • Wall closets and under-pod storage for personal items
  • Three daily meals and full retreat program included

Youth Learning Center

Parents may drop off their kids (6-18 years) at the dedicated Youth Learning Center, where trained facilitators provide attentive guidance and supervision. The program includes age-appropriate meditations, nature hikes, Adventure Park activities, and even special visits from Cirak.

To preserve the retreat’s atmosphere of Noble Silence and deep inner work, children remain within their own program and do not mingle with the main group or parents (except in urgent situations). This creates a supportive environment for both adults and youth — allowing parents to fully immerse in their own journey, while children engage in meaningful growth and exploration in parallel.

All-inclusive ₹11,250/child
