ABOUT CIRAK

 

Cirak is a spiritual teacher, writer, and speaker dedicated to helping people create meaning in their everyday lives. Blending ancient wisdom with modern practicality, Cirak offers a fresh, no-nonsense approach to spirituality—one that isn’t about chasing enlightenment but about understanding the design of life and making better decisions.

Through books, workshops, and live teachings, Cirak guides you toward clarity, purpose, and a deeper connection to the present moment. His work is for those seeking more than surface-level answers—those ready to see life through a sharper lens and step into a deeper, more meaningful way of living.

Primarily influenced by Eckhart Tolle, Alan Watts, J. Krishnamurti, Thich Nhat Hanh, Osho, Ram Daas, Deepak Chopra, Bob Proctor, and Wayne Dyer, Cirak divides his time between writing books, hosting workshops and retreats, and giving talks on mindfulness.

He is a certified meditation teacher, trained in Reiki and Ba Duan Jin Qi Gong, and a graduate and facilitator of the Eckhart Tolle School of Awakening. He is also available for private energy clearing and life coaching sessions.

KEY TAKEAWAYS

 

You will learn how to navigate life with clarity, purpose, and a sense of ease. This Masterclass will equip you with deep insights, practical tools, and interactive discussions.

  • Stop reacting to life and start responding with intention.
  • Break free from the mental loops that keep you stuck.
  • See beyond surface-level problems to the deeper patterns at play.
  • Make decisions with clarity, confidence, and purpose.
  • Find meaning in every moment—not just in big milestones.

This Masterclass is for those ready to step beyond the ordinary, see life through a sharper lens, and cultivate a sense of meaning that is not dependent on circumstances. Whether you feel stuck, uninspired, or simply want to experience life more deeply, this is your opportunity to reframe your understanding of what truly matters.

If you are ready to stop searching and start living, this is where it begins.
