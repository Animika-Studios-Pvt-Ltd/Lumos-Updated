Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Palowise India platform and partnership.

Who is Palowise?
Palowise is an award-winning European AI-powered social media intelligence and analytics company headquartered in Athens. We help organizations turn digital conversations and online content into clear, actionable brand insight.
Who is Lumos?
Lumos is a Bengaluru-based digital marketing solutions and technology consulting agency. We support leading brands, agencies, and organizations in establishing their digital presence, executing strategic communications, and implementing high-impact campaigns.
What does the partnership with Lumos mean for Indian clients?
It means that Indian brands, agencies, and organizations can access the full Palowise platform with dedicated local representation and support from Lumos, including onboarding, custom integration, training, and strategic guidance.
Which capabilities are available in India?
Indian clients can access sentiment and emotion analysis, topic and theme analysis, competitive intelligence, trend detection, influencer analytics, reputation monitoring, and broader web and social intelligence, all through a single dashboard.
Who is this solution best suited for?
The solution is ideal for brands, agencies, and organizations that need timely, high-fidelity insight to guide marketing, communications, reputation management, and strategic decision-making in the fast-moving Indian market.
How do we get started?
You can book a demo or speak directly with our India team via Lumos. We will walk you through the platform, discuss your specific needs, and recommend an onboarding path that fits your team.
